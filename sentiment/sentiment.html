What This Project Does

This application scrapes social media discussions about any topic, then uses AI to analyze sentiment trends and extract actionable insights that would normally require hours of manual research.

🤔 In Simple Terms

Imagine you're a company that needs to know what people are saying about your industry, competitors, or trending topics.

The challenge: Manually reading through hundreds of social media posts and determining overall sentiment takes days of work.

This tool: Automatically scrapes Reddit discussions and uses AI to analyze sentiment, categorize themes, and generate insights in minutes instead of hours.
