What This Project Does

This is a Python bot that automatically identifies overdue equipment and sends personalized reminder emails to borrowers, saving my supervisor 5 hours per week.

ü§î In Simple Terms

The Situation: A department at a university loans out equipment (laptops, cameras, tools) to staff and students.

The Problem: My supervisor had to manually check an Excel spreadsheet, figure out what was overdue, look up contact information, and send individual emails to each person. Keeping track of what's overdue and emailing reminders was taking 5+ hours of manual work every week.

My Solution: This bot automates this process - it reads the spreadsheet, identifies overdue items, and sends personalized emails all by simply running the program once a week or whatever the desired frequency of emails are.
