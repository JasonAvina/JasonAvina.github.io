What This Project Does

I built a computer program that can predict how diabetes will progress in patients. This program is for demo purposes only.

ü§î In Simple Terms

Imagine you're a doctor with 442 diabetes patients. You have basic information about each patient: their age, weight, blood pressure, and some blood test results.

The challenge: Can you predict which patients will get worse faster? This helps you prioritize care and catch problems early.

My solution: I trained 6 different AI models to make these predictions, then compared them to find the best approach.

‚ùå The Problem

Doctors often have to make treatment decisions without knowing exactly how fast a patient's diabetes will progress. This can lead to:

  • Late interventions

  • Missed opportunities for prevention

‚úÖ My Solution
