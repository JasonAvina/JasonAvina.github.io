What This Project Does

This application simulates a clinical trial comparing ACE inhibitors vs Beta blockers for blood pressure reduction, then performs statistical hypothesis testing to determine if there's a significant difference between treatments.

🤔 In Simple Terms

Imagine you're a hospital data scientist tasked with analyzing the effectiveness of two blood pressure medications in clinical trials.

The challenge: Given patient data from two treatment groups, can you determine if one medication is statistically significantly better than the other?

This tool: Simulates realistic clinical trial data and performs Welch's two-sample t-test to make statistically sound conclusions.
